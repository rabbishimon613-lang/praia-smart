# Revenue model — paid venue placement

## What we sell

Paid placement on the agito (movement) section of each beach card. Surfaces venues at the user's decision moment — when they're choosing where to go *right now*.

## Tiers

| Tier | Who | Gets | Monthly price (R$) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free directory** | Any beachside venue | Plain listing: name, type, hours, IG link | 0 |
| **Verified** | Owner-claimed listings | Photo, menu, "claimed ✓" badge, edit access | 0 |
| **Featured** | Paid placement | Top slot in agito section, "Recomendado" label, vibe pill | 300–800 |
| **Event spotlight** | One-off | Pinned event card for the week | 50–200 / event |
| **Multi-beach pack** | Chains | Featured at all locations + analytics dashboard | 2.000–5.000 |

## Slot scarcity rules

- **1 featured slot per posto per category** (one bar, one restaurant, one hotel per posto)
- Slots renew month-to-month; no annual lock-in
- First-mover gets launch pricing; renewals at insider rate

## ROI math (sample — Pavão Azul, Copacabana)

Pavão Azul ~R$80k/mo gross summer. To make R$500/mo placement obviously worth it, we need to add ≥R$3k/mo (6× return).

| Revenue lever | Monthly bump (R$) | Math |
|---|---|---|
| Tourist discovery (Dec–Feb) | 8.000–12.000 | 30 extra parties × R$300 avg ticket |
| Filled off-peak hours | 3.000–5.000 | 4 extra covers × 60 days |
| Bypassed Booking/iFood commissions | 1.000–2.000 | 20 reservations × ~R$100 saved fee |
| **Total realistic upside** | **12.000–19.000** | Pays the featured slot 25–40× over |

## Order of operations (do NOT skip)

1. **Phase 1 — Free directory** (build content, no sales yet)
   - Manually populate ~10 venues per top-traffic beach
   - Pull from Google Maps, write 1-line notes
   - Looks legit, helps SEO via venue-name keywords
2. **Phase 2 — Owner-claim flow** (still free, builds the email list)
   - Per-listing "reivindicar grátis" form
   - Verified listings get a green check + bigger photo
   - Owner emails captured for future Phase 3 pitch
3. **Phase 3 — Featured upsell**
   - Only start sales calls after Phase 2 has 50+ claimed listings AND traffic data
   - Pitch references real data: "your listing got X views last month, mostly from search Y"

Cold-emailing now (before Phase 1) = looks like spam, kills domain reputation.

## Adjacent revenue streams (parked)

- **Beach event aggregator** — pull from Eventbrite Brasil + RA + scrape-friendly sources. Charge events for spotlight. Each event has urgency = higher conversion than evergreen venue placement.
- **Affiliate links** — Booking, Hurb, Airbnb for hotel bookings near beaches. Smaller take but zero sales work.
- **Branded content** — sponsored "Pra surfar" hero card. Cervejas, sunscreen brands, surf wax. Bigger ticket once traffic justifies.
