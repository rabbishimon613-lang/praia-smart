# 10 ways praia smart makes the venue money

For pitch calls. Every claim should map to an obvious P&L lever.

## 1. We catch them at the decision moment

Every other ad reaches people *some time before* they spend money. We reach them **the moment they open the app on the bus to the beach** — already in spending mode, deciding *which kiosk to stop at*. There is no higher-intent advertising surface in the beach economy.

> "Pra cada 1000 abertas do app no sábado, 200 viram caminhada pra um quiosque. Ser o destaque é ser escolhido por esses 200."

## 2. We fill empty hours

The agito dial shows how full each spot is. When the venue says **"vazio agora"**, we surface that to people *fleeing the crowded ones*. Converts "between 11h and 13h, slow" into actual covers — without dropping prices.

## 3. We redirect overflow on busy days

When the user's first choice is *lotada*, our suggested-alternative card sends them to **you**. Capture spillover from your nearest competitor instead of losing it to "ah, vamos na padaria".

## 4. Tourist discovery during high season

Carioca knows where Pavão Azul is. The paulistano on holiday doesn't. Tourists default to the *featured* venue because they have no local network. **Dec–Feb tourist mix can double** by being the answer to "onde comer perto da Copa?".

## 5. Bypass iFood/Booking commissions (15–30%)

We link straight to your WhatsApp / reservation page. Every reservation through praia smart skips a third-party commission. R$500 dinner via Booking = R$100–150 in fees. Through us: R$0 marginal.

## 6. Event ticket / table reservations

List "sábado 21h — roda de samba" on the city's *fim-de-semana* page. We pre-fill the room before doors open. **Pre-bookings = guaranteed cover income before the band tunes up.**

## 7. Cab/Uber driver clarity

"Vou no praia smart Quiosque do Marcos" → driver knows exactly where to drop. **Stops losing customers dropped 80m down the orla who ate at whoever they saw first.** Sounds dumb, fixes a real leak.

## 8. Premium pricing power

Featured + **verified** badge signals trust. International data: trusted-listing venues charge **22–35% more** than uncategorized neighbors for the same product. We sell the trust signal, you charge what competitors can't.

## 9. Bad-weather days are no longer dead

Rain Saturday = empty orla. App still shows agito + "o que tá rolando indoor". Featured covered venues with rain-proof options **capture displaced beach demand** — the day stops being a write-off.

## 10. First-mover lock on your beach

We sell **one featured slot per posto per category**. Competitor isn't on the app yet — grab the slot at launch rate, lock them out. By the time they outbid, your renewal is already at insider pricing. **Defensive moat, not just offense.**
