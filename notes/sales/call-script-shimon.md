# Shimon's 20-minute video-call agenda

For paid-placement pitch calls with venue owners. Renata books, Shimon closes.

## Pre-call prep (5 min)

- Pull the venue's Google Maps reviews (volume, ★, sentiment, photo count)
- Check their Instagram (followers, post cadence, what they post — drinks? music? food?)
- Note their nearest competitor on the same posto
- Open praia smart on the beach in question — show during call

## Call structure (target: 18 min, leave buffer)

### 0:00 — 2:00 · Warm-up
- Renata intros, hands to Shimon
- Shimon's opener: *"Antes de mostrar a plataforma, me conta uma coisa — qual o dia da semana mais fraco de vocês na temporada?"*
- Listen. The answer tells you which lever to pitch hardest (off-peak hours? tourist mix? bad-weather days?)

### 2:00 — 6:00 · Show, don't tell
- Share screen, open `praiasmart.com/{beach-slug}/fim-de-semana`
- Walk through what users see: live cam, conditions, água-limpa, melhor horário, agito
- Point at the agito section: *"Essa parte aqui é onde a gente quer colocar vocês."*
- Zoom on a competitor card if relevant — show how a featured slot would look

### 6:00 — 11:00 · The 10-ways pitch (compressed)

Don't read all 10. Pick the 2-3 that match what they said in 0:00.

If they said "weekday lunch dies" → lead with **#2 fill empty hours** + **#3 redirect overflow**.
If they said "tourists don't find us" → lead with **#4 tourist discovery** + **#8 premium pricing**.
If they said "Booking takes too much" → lead with **#5 bypass commissions** + **#6 event reservations**.
If they're worried about rain Mondays → **#9 bad-weather days**.

End with **#10 first-mover lock** every time. Scarcity closes.

### 11:00 — 14:00 · The math

Pull up the ROI table from `value-prop-for-venues.md`. Walk through:
- Their estimated monthly gross (ask, don't guess)
- Realistic bump per lever for THEIR venue type
- Featured price R$500/mo
- "Cada cover paga seu mês"

### 14:00 — 17:00 · The ask

> *"Tô oferecendo 30 dias de teste no slot Recomendado do {posto}. R$500 pelo primeiro mês. Se em 30 dias você não viu retorno, a gente desliga e não tem contrato pra cumprir. Topa?"*

If yes → next steps (Renata sends contract, pix details, listing setup)
If maybe → "qual a dúvida específica?" — answer 1-2, then re-ask
If no → "tudo bem — posso te perguntar o porquê, só pra eu aprender?"

### 17:00 — 20:00 · Wrap

- Ask if they know any other venues that should be on praia smart (warm-intro pipeline)
- Confirm Renata's WhatsApp for follow-up
- Thank, end

## What NOT to do on the call

- Don't mention competitor venues by name (city's small, owners know each other)
- Don't oversell tech (cam-first, light theme, etc.) — they don't care
- Don't promise specific traffic numbers we can't deliver yet
- Don't drop price below R$500 to close — it sets a floor
- Don't talk for more than 90s straight — Brazilians read silence as pressure

## Objection handling

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "Não tenho orçamento agora" | "Entendi. Posso fechar uma reserva de slot sem cobrar até dezembro? Aí você decide quando ativar." |
| "Quero ver dados primeiro" | "Justíssimo. Vou te mandar o printout do tráfego do {beach} dos últimos 30 dias. Posso ligar de novo na sexta?" |
| "Vou conversar com meu sócio" | "Claro. Quando seria uma boa hora pra eu retornar — segunda? Te mando lembrete no WhatsApp." |
| "Outra plataforma já me ofereceu" | "Top, qual? [Listen.] A gente é diferente porque a pessoa abre o app NO momento de ir pra praia. Outras plataformas pegam o usuário muito antes ou muito depois." |
| "E se eu não gostar?" | "30 dias, cancela quando quiser, sem multa. Eu confio no produto. Você?" |

## Post-call (within 2 hours)

- Renata sends recap email + next-step (contract link or follow-up time)
- Shimon notes in Notion / Airtable: outcome, next step, key quote
- If "yes": activate listing within 24h, before they cool off

## Targets

- Week 1: 5 calls booked, 2 closes
- Week 4: 20 calls booked, 8 closes (40% close rate is ambitious but reasonable B2B)
- Month 3: 50 paid slots active → R$25k/mo recurring
