## Notes on tone

- Formal-warm Brazilian business standard. *Prezado(a)*, *gostaríamos*, *fico no aguardo* signal professionalism without stiffness.
- "está no nosso mapeamento estratégico" makes the venue feel chosen, not blasted.
- No money mentioned. Sales math comes on the video call. Email's only job is to book the meeting.
- Two CTAs: suggest times OR WhatsApp. Brazilians strongly prefer WhatsApp — make it easy to bypass email.
- NYC framing adds foreign-backed credibility without sounding imported.

## Follow-up cadence (when implementing)

- Day 0: send email
- Day 3: WhatsApp message (1-liner)
- Day 7: second email (different subject, lighter touch)
- Day 14: drop. Move on.
